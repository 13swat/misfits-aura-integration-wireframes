💡 Hybrid Consideration: Misfits + Aura + Cards

Combine Aura's retention mechanics with Cards' virality. Aura becomes the underlying points system, cards become the shareable output.

• Misfits Clubs as the foundation (existing infrastructure)
• Earn aura from club activities → Aura milestones unlock card reveals
• Friend circle aura = casual card generation ("Today's vibe" cards)
• Profile aura = milestone cards (achievement unlocks)
• IRL cards = physical manifestation of digital aura milestones

Weight Calculation

Factors marked ×3 are weighted 3x (K-Factor, Daily Habit, IRL Bridge, Ease of Execution).
Factors marked ×2 are weighted 2x.
Unmarked factors are weighted 1x.
Max possible weighted score: 120 points.
